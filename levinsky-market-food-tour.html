It is a private or group walking tour in Levinsky Market, Tel Aviv, guided in English or Hebrew. The tour combines food tastings, spice shops, herbs, infusions, Ayurveda, Persian-Iranian heritage and food culture for visitors, teams, senior groups and wellness-focused groups.

FAQ

Is the tour available in English?

Yes. The tour can be held in English or Hebrew and is suitable for English-speaking groups, native English-speaking visitors, international guests, corporate teams, private groups and visitors to Tel Aviv.

Is this a food tour or a herbal medicine tour?

It is both: food culture, tastings and market stories, with a VEDICA angle that includes Ayurveda, Persian-Iranian heritage, Iranian food culture, wellness, infusions and practical home use of spices and herbs.

Who is the tour for?

English-speaking groups, private groups, senior groups, corporate teams, offsites, client hosting, international visitors, wellness groups, health-focused groups and curious food lovers.

Can the tour work for native English-speaking groups?

Yes. The page is built for groups that want a clear English-speaking market experience, including native English-speaking visitors, mixed international teams and guests who prefer the tour to be guided in English.

What practical details should we send before booking?

Send the number of participants, preferred date or date range, preferred language, dietary needs, group type, the purpose of the tour and whether the group wants the optional lunch bundle. Duration, meeting point and route can then be confirmed according to the group.

Can dietary needs be considered?

Yes. Share food sensitivities, allergies, kosher needs or other dietary considerations before booking so the route and tastings can be planned responsibly where possible.

What is included in the Levinsky Market tour?

The experience includes English or Hebrew guidance through Levinsky Market, an Ayurvedic infusion, snacks and an introductory tasting kit. Additional stops and tastings vary with the group, season, route and vendor availability.

How much does the tour cost?

The individual price is 220 NIS per participant. For groups, the price is 180 NIS per participant. Groups can request an added lunch bundle on demand; lunch bundle details and pricing are confirmed separately.

What should we send to request a group quote?

Send the estimated number of participants, preferred date or date range, preferred language, food sensitivities, the purpose of the tour and whether you want the optional lunch bundle.

How long is the tour and where does it start?

The route, duration and exact Levinsky Market meeting point are confirmed after booking according to group size, pace, vendor availability and the goals of the visit.

Can the pace or accessibility needs be adapted?

Yes. Share mobility needs, preferred pace, senior group considerations or accessibility questions before booking so the route can be planned as responsibly as possible.

Is the tour medical advice?

No. The tour is an educational food, culture and wellness experience. It discusses traditional uses of herbs, spices and infusions in a grounded way, but it does not replace personal medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.
