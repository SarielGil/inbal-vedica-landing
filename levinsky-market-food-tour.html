What happens on the tour?

We walk through Levinsky Market, meet the world of local spice shops and food traditions, and explore how everyday kitchen ingredients are understood in Ayurveda and Persian medicine.

The tour can include tastings, an Ayurvedic infusion, stories from Iranian and Indian food culture, and practical ideas for using spices and herbs at home in a grounded, non-medical way.

It works well as a private food tour, a corporate team activity, client hosting, an educational wellness session or a cultural experience for guests visiting Tel Aviv.

FAQ

Is the tour available in English?

Yes. The tour can be held in English or Hebrew and is suitable for international guests, corporate teams, private groups and visitors to Tel Aviv.

Is this a food tour or a herbal medicine tour?

It is both: food culture, tastings and market stories, with a VEDICA angle that includes Ayurveda, Persian medicine, Iranian food culture, infusions and practical home use of spices and herbs.

Who is the tour for?

Private groups, corporate teams, offsites, client hosting, international visitors, wellness groups and curious food lovers.
