## 🔗 Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub Pages (High Priority)
- **Index** → links to all main sections
- **Clinic** → links to About, Contact, Workshops, Tours
- **About** → links to Clinic, Blog, Contact
- **Blog** → links to Clinic, Workshops, Index

### Content Linking
- Each page has 3-5 contextual internal links
- "Learn more" links point to related services
- CTAs always link to Clinic or Contact

### Breadcrumb Structure
```
Home > About > [Current Page]
Home > Clinic > [Related Content]
Home > Blog > [Category] > [Post]
```
