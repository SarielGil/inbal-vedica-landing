## Recommendations

1. The home page is currently the strongest organic page and should remain the entity hub.
2. The week-over-week CTR dipped from 7.5% to 5.4% and average position softened from 5.74 to 6.18, but impressions were stable. Watch another week before calling it a regression.
3. Add or preserve direct internal links from the hero/top section to the highest-value conversion pages: Hebrew clinic, English clinic and contact/WhatsApp.
4. Make sure the first visible copy keeps the exact entity triangle clear: `ענבל הנסב`, `VEDICA`, `רפואה איורוודית ופרסית בתל אביב`.
5. Keep external trust profiles synchronized with the same entity language: Infomed, Easy, Nizat, Instagram/Facebook and any Google Business Profile.
6. Consider adding `sameAs` references to every strong external profile in the Organization/Person schema if any are missing or outdated.
